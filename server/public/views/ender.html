Ender, the Xenocide

Many have struggled against difficult conditions, but few have overcome as much as Ender. He was a third child in a time where only two children were allowed by law to slow population growth - he entered life as a target for hatred, bringing it not just upon himself but his whole family.

However, the challenges he faced as a very young child prepared him for his pre-teen years, as he was trained to command Earth's entire fleet in the ongoing fight against the Formics (also known as the Buggers), an ant-like alien race that had almost destroyed humanity.
